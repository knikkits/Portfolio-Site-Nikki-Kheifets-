My Story

I am a Junior UX Designer based in Vancouver, BC. I have always had a passion for art and design as well as the psychology behind why people visualize and learn thing differently. I love creating projects and learning new techniques along the way during development. Since I knew how to use a computer I have been creating projects as gifts for family and friends with Adobe Photoshop. At my current role I am the designated IT person, due to my logic in problem solving with technology. I used to work as an education assistant and behaviour interventionist. I have always been so interested in how the mind of different people and different age groups developed. Discovering digital design and combining it with a concepts of user friendly information architecture has catered to my wants and needs for a career. I am excited to continue learning and developing my skills as a UX Designer.
