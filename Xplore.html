Xploration App

"live your city · love your city"

Xploration is an app for those who have lived in the same are for a while and want to learn more about their surrounding, and/or for those who just moved somewhere and are looking to explore the new area. The app is for anyone who wants to go out to eat at a new place, hike or walk around exploring new views, go dance the night away, or simply meet some locals. Xploration lets you chat, plan activities and make memories with your friends or new people with similar interests. The app allows users to be active and learn about the excitement around them that they did not know existed. This is for users that live in the city to love their city. This project has been worked on from June to December of 2019.


The app logo is an abstract representation of 2 pin drop icons forming the shape of a heart with a third pin drop on top to represent the location of love. This is where the quote "live your city, love your city comes from". The idea here is the more one learns about where they live the more they will learn to love it. The colour purple is a combination of the calm stability of blue with the energy of red. This represents the ease of finding something new to do while riddled with excitement. Purple may also stand for ambition; Xploration is meant for users to step outside of their comfort zone and set goals to try new things. On the home page users are recommended maps they can explore that have been most popular amonsgt other users in the area.

The main feature of the app is the map feature. Here the user is able to search their location and see what hunts are available in the area. They can then read a little bit about the different stop locations and decide whether they would like to save this map for later, start it right away, or chose something else!

If you are happy with your selection, you may chose to save the map in the app's calendar. As well, you have the opportunity to meat other users online who may share the same interests. This way you can complete the maps with an existing or new friend. The app allows the users to communicate though a chat, and add each other through their personal profiles.

The app will inform the user how to check into each location. Once checked in the user can chose to explore the area, stay for a while or move on to the next stop! They are also able to add photos and reviews from their experience to their profile.

If the user choses to stay at a location, they can finish the map on another day. It is a success if you are having fun! There is a point incentive if maps are completed. Points can add up to discounts at a variety of locations that the user has visited during their exploration. The biggest incentive however is finding new places to visit, meeting new people, and making everylasting memories.
